Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SwipeSweep

General Questions

What is SwipeSweep?

SwipeSweep is an iOS photo management app that uses intuitive swipe gestures to help you quickly organize and clean your photo library. Think of it as "Tinder for photos" - swipe left to delete, right to keep, and up to favorite.

How much storage space can I free up?

Users typically free up 2-8GB of storage space, with some users reclaiming over 15GB. The amount depends on your photo library size and how many duplicates, screenshots, and unwanted photos you have.

Is SwipeSweep free to use?

Yes, SwipeSweep is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or in-app purchases required.

Privacy & Safety

Is my data safe with SwipeSweep?

Absolutely. SwipeSweep processes all photos locally on your device. Your photos never leave your iPhone, and we don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or images.

Can I recover deleted photos?

Yes! SwipeSweep includes an undo function for recent actions. Additionally, deleted photos go to your iPhone's "Recently Deleted" album where they can be recovered for 30 days.

What permissions does SwipeSweep need?

SwipeSweep only requires access to your photo library to function. We don't request access to your contacts, location, camera, or any other sensitive data.

Features

How do the swipe gestures work?

It's simple: swipe left to delete a photo, swipe right to keep it, and swipe up to mark it as a favorite. The gestures are designed to be intuitive and fast.

Can I select multiple photos at once?

Yes! SwipeSweep supports batch operations. You can select multiple photos and perform bulk actions like delete, keep, or favorite.

Does SwipeSweep detect duplicate photos?

Yes, SwipeSweep can identify and help you remove duplicate and similar photos, freeing up significant storage space.

Troubleshooting

The app isn't showing my photos

Make sure you've granted SwipeSweep permission to access your photo library. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos and ensure SwipeSweep is set to "All Photos".

SwipeSweep is running slowly

If you have a very large photo library (10,000+ photos), the initial scan may take some time. Try restarting the app or your device if performance issues persist.

I accidentally deleted important photos

Don't panic! Check your iPhone's "Recently Deleted" album first. Photos stay there for 30 days. You can also use SwipeSweep's undo function if the deletion was recent.
