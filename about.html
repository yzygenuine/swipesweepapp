About SwipeSweep

Simplifying photo management, one swipe at a time

Our Story

SwipeSweep was born from a simple frustration: managing thousands of photos on our iPhones was tedious and time-consuming. Traditional photo apps required multiple taps and complex interfaces to organize photos.

We asked ourselves: "What if organizing photos could be as simple and intuitive as swiping on a dating app?" That question led to the creation of SwipeSweep - the first photo management app to use Tinder-like swipe gestures.

Since launching in 2024, SwipeSweep has helped over 50,000 users reclaim more than 250TB of storage space across their devices.
